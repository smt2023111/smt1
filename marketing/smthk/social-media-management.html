Frequently Asked Questions

Is keyword research important?

Keyword research is crucial for SEO and paid ads as it deeply impacts target audience and business opportunities. Our team uses latest tools and techniques to thoroughly analyze the most relevant and high converting keywords in customer's industry and recommend them.

What's the difference between SEO and paid advertising?

SEO helps customers' websites rank higher organically in search results. Paid advertising drives users to websites through ads on search results,social platforms, etc. Though SEO takes time, it helps with long term growth while paid advertising is relatively quick. Our approach combines both to provide a holistic data-driven growth solution.

How do you improve ad efficiency?

We use A/B split testing to constantly optimize ad headlines, copy and targeting to find the most effective ad setups. At the same time, we utilize data analytics to understand user behavior and interactions to continuously improve ad promotion strategies.
